A

Recommended Design

Adopt convex-hull seed plus planar visibility ear insertion with a local min-angle filter. It is effectively a graph-formalized safe-dent polygonizer, keeps all sampled points in the batch, and nearly saturates the closed-cubic gate.

B

Conservative Alternative

Use stricter hull-dents that may drop unsafe points. It is simpler to reason about, but leaves more cubic crossings and has lower acceptance.
