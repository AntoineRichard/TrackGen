\section{Domain Constraints}
\label{sec:domain-constraints}

Domain constraints are analyzed separately for ground, aerial, and maritime racing
before their common structure is compared across domains.

\subsection{Ground}

The ground-domain analysis covers surface-bound geometry, vehicle clearance, and
route-boundary constraints.

\subsection{Aerial}

The aerial-domain analysis covers three-dimensional gate placement, traversal
orientation, and free-space constraints.

\subsection{Maritime}

The maritime-domain analysis covers buoy-defined routes, navigable regions, and
environmental constraints represented by course generators.

\subsection{Cross-Domain}

The cross-domain analysis identifies constraints that can be expressed consistently
across vehicle classes without erasing domain-specific requirements.
