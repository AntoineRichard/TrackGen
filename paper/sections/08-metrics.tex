\section{Metrics and Reporting Protocol}
\label{sec:metrics}

The metric protocol separates validity checks from descriptive and comparative
measurements so that each reported quantity has a clear role.

\subsection{Feasibility}

Feasibility metrics assess whether a generated course satisfies its stated constraints.

\subsection{Geometry}

Geometry metrics describe the spatial and topological properties of a course.

\subsection{Difficulty}

Difficulty metrics characterize measurable sources of task demand without assuming a
fixed set of categorical levels.

\subsection{Diversity}

Diversity metrics compare variation within and between course collections.

\subsection{Reproducibility}

Reproducibility reporting records the generator configuration, randomization controls,
software state, and retained artifacts needed to reconstruct a course collection.

\subsection{Simulation Feasibility}

Simulation-feasibility metrics assess whether a represented course can be instantiated
and executed in the target simulation workflow.
