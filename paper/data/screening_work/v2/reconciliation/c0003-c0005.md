- **Section 3, `TORCS and Speed Dreams`:** identifies TORCS as a car-racing

  ordered segment representation, with straight and turn parameters, and a

  genotype-to-phenotype mapping.

  that the backend maps genotypes to phenotypes, creates downloadable tracks,
  and builds the actual TORCS/Speed Dreams package through elevation, terrain,
  and track-decoration procedures.

packages. The simulator/dynamics statement supplies the protocol-relevant
mapping from the source's racing-game tracks to physics-based simulated-vehicle
courses. On that evidence, the accountable author can now assess the first
applicable criterion as **provisionally `included / include-1`**.

`excluded / exclude-insufficient-detail`** assessment required by the protocol;
the positive inclusion claim lacks material evidence.
